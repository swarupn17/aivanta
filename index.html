Who We Are

Why Aivanta

Different by design

A traditional olympiad asks "who is the best?" We ask "is every child prepared for the future?"

  • Voluntary participation — pick any one, two or all three subjects. No pressure, ever.
  • No hidden charges — one fee of ₹150 per subject, study material included.
  • Equal access — government, private, urban and rural schools all welcome.
  • Real-world focus — awareness, skills and values over rote memorisation.
