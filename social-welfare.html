Our Programs

Putting the fund to work

Merit Scholarships

Cash scholarships for top students from government schools — with preference for girls and rural students.

Free Awareness Workshops

Sessions on financial fraud, cyber safety and AI literacy in Zilla Parishad and rural schools.

Free Study-Material Kits

Printed kits for students from economically weaker sections whose families cannot afford the fee.

Community Awareness Camps

UPI fraud, digital safety and government schemes — for parents and families in rural areas.

Teacher Training

Equipping government school teachers to deliver financial and cyber awareness in their own classrooms.

Women & Girls Empowerment

Sessions on financial independence, digital banking and online safety across villages.
